Gratud

UX / UI design for ecommerce

Wishlist platform with a social sense, where you can make monetary donations, in products sold by the same platform and even give tips.

Profile Client

Gratud is an ecommerce with a wishlist sales format which aims to make it easier to make donations either monetary or in product to users of the platform

Goal

The main task was to create a platform that could mix the idea of an ecommerce, a wish list with crowdfunding. And that it was easy to understand and use.

Plan

My approach was to create a platform that would provide accurate information on the dona- tions or products you want and that would take just a few steps to com- plete the donationt.

What we achive

We achieved a platform that is able to make diffe- rent types of donations, where the user is always in control of what he will donate and how he will do it.

Information architecture

As it was necessary to have a platform with a navigation similar to that of a social network and an ecommerce, we created this information architecture in which the user could understand where he was and what information he could find there.

Wireframes

The wireframe process was intense, several versions of the navigation were created to create a logical user journey that was simple to understand and easy to execute.
